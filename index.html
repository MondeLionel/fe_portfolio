Gong

Gong is a platform that aims to bring local and international audiences exciting and emotionally engaging stories through old and new mediums.

The audience will skew younger towards digital natives and savvy internet users. Exploring African stories through historical lens for the modern audience. Stories exploring depth beyond simple-minded tropes like "Kasi gangster", "Upper-middle class crime family", "Screaming warriors" and "Hyper-sexual blacks".

  1. Actively find hidden talents.
  2. Build a custom platform to augment existing platforms in order to control both quality and flow of analytical data while actively seeking a way to monetize content through partnerships.
  3. Providing a proper springboard for small, well-written stories/comics/music in and around KZN.
